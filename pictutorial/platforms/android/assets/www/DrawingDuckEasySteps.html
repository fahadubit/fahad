How To Draw Duck in Simple 5 Steps

In this drawing lesson we will show you how to draw a Duck in 5 easy steps. This step by step lesson progressively builds upon each previous step until you get to the final rendering of the Duck.

Here are some interesting facts about the Duck you might find interesting.

Most ducks exploit a variety of food sources such as grasses, aquatic plants, fish, insects, small amphibians, worms, and small molluscs.

  1. The ducks are generally monogamous, although these bonds generally last a single year only.
  2. Despite widespread misconceptions, only the females of most dabbling ducks “quack”.
  3. The ducks have a cosmopolitan distribution occurring across most of the world except for Antarctica.
  4. Ducks cannot feel the cold in their feet even when swimming in icy cold water.
